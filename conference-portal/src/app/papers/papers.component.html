Call for Papers

InteRact receives proposals for papers in the following formats: full paper, short paper and poster, which will be reviewed in blind review. Papers have the opportunity to be published in the Symposium Proceeding (in digital format). The first authors of papers can be students from different levels of higher education – BSc, MSc, PhD or others. If you submit: Deliver paper

Review Process:

Paper submission Blind review Notified about the reviewing process

General information for submitting papers for review:

Papers must follow the template Papers can be written in Portuguese or English Papers must be submitted in ".pdf" format.

Specific information for paper submission:

Full Paper

Full papers must be original. Submissions must be limited to a minimum number of 6 pages and a maximum number of 8 A4 pages.

 Short Papers

Short papers should describe work in progress but with relevant preliminary results to be discussed. .

 Posters

Posters should describe work in progress, preferably including some preliminary results.

How can I submit an paper?

 Step 1 First, you must download the paper template (LNCS). Step 2 All papers must be submitted through the “EasyChair” platform. Step 3 After logging into your account, you must select the “New Submission” option and fill the form. Step 4 Finally, you must upload your paper and submit. Afterwards, you will receive an e-mail with confirmation that your paper was successfully submitted.

General information for final paper submission:

 Papers must follow the template available at LNCS. Papers can be written in Portuguese or English. The final version of the papers must be submitted in a “.zip” file, which must contain. The final version must be submitted through the EasyChair platform.

Recommendations for poster design:

 It must be self-contained and self-explanatory, allowing the reader. It should be simple and clear, with a combination of text, graphics and images being recommended. It must allow the reading of images and graphics from a distance. It must have a highlighted area for the title, introduction, conclusions and authors. It must be vertically oriented and the recommended size is A1. There will be a place to stick the poster during the sessions.

Outline about the Presentations:

 Each author of the full paper will have a maximum of 15 minutes to presen. Each author of the short paper will have a maximum of 10.
